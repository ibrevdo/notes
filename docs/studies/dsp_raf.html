Inner product

x = (X0,...Xn-1), y = (Y0, ..., Yn-1)

(x,y) = SUM(Xn,Yn) when n=0 to N-1

Angle between two vectors: cos(teta)=(x,y)/ (||x|| ||y||) teta is angle betwwen two vectors when: ||x||^2 = SUM(Xi^2), i=0 to N-1 (Norma of x)

Parallel vectors : teta = 0, cos(teta) = 1 xIy , teta = pi/2 , cos(teta) = 0 if vectors are normalized, (x,y) = cos(teta)

Projection

a - is a projection of vector x on y a = ||x|| cos(teta)
